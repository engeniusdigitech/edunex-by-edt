Detailed Feature Coverage

Core ERP Modules

EduNex ERP covers the full institute workflow, from student admission to fee collection, teaching, exams, reports, and staff management.

Student Management

  • Student profile, contact, parent details, batch assignment, and profile image.
  • Admission and student record maintenance.
  • Student-wise academic, attendance, fee, and report history.

Batch and Subject Management

  • Create batches/classes with schedules and active status.
  • Assign subjects to batches.
  • Map teachers to subjects and class teacher responsibility.

Attendance Management

  • Daily student attendance marking.
  • Class teacher-controlled attendance workflow.
  • Present/absent summary, attendance percentage, and PDF reports.

Fees and Payments

  • Fee categories, structures, due dates, allocations, and student fees.
  • Payment ledger, receipts, balance tracking, and recent payments.
  • Defaulter reports and WhatsApp/portal reminders.

Online Payment Gateway

  • Payment gateway settings for institute accounts.
  • Student fee checkout support.
  • Receipt download after successful payment.

Reports and Analytics

  • Revenue overview, attendance trend, students by batch, and KPIs.
  • Attendance PDFs, defaulter PDFs, and student reports.
  • Useful dashboards for admin, teacher, student, and super admin.

Academics and Learning

Academic Features

Teachers and academic heads get a structured workflow for classes, homework, tests, live lectures, timetable, and student progress.

Homework

  • Create homework for selected batches and subjects.
  • Add due dates and instructions.
  • Students can view homework from the mobile app/student portal.

Tests and Exams

  • Schedule tests with subject, batch, date, and total marks.
  • Fast mark entry for complete batches.
  • Publish marks, remarks, and results to students.

Live Lectures

  • Create live lecture sessions for selected batches.
  • Teacher starts and ends live classes.
  • Students access live and ended lecture records from the app.

Timetable

  • Build class-wise timetable slots.
  • Teachers can view their own schedule.
  • Students can view batch timetable from mobile app/student portal.

Leave Management

  • Staff leave requests and student leave requests.
  • Review, approve, reject, and revert decisions.
  • Class teacher/principal/admin review support.

Discipline and Gallery

  • Record student discipline details.
  • Maintain image gallery for institute updates.
  • Students can view gallery and discipline information.
