The Engine Under the Hood

Integrated Batch Ecosystem

Everything revolves around Batches. A batch represents a class or cohort. Once a batch is created:

Subject Mapping

Assign unlimited subjects to each batch. Map specific teachers to specific subjects.

Student Allocation

Students are grouped into batches, ensuring their dashboard remains relevant to their class.

The Class Teacher Security

Attendance is locked down for maximum integrity. No one can mark attendance except the designated Class Teacher.

  • Admins appoint teachers to batches as "Class Teachers".
  • That teacher then sees a "My Class" toggle on their dashboard.
  • Interactive charts show real-time "Present vs Absent" rings.
  • Monthly PDF reports can be exported for any class.

Automated Fee Collection

EduNex eliminates manual billing errors with its multi-tier fee structure logic.

Flow:
Create Fee Category (e.g., Monthly Tuition) -> Define Fee Structure (Amount, Due Date) -> Assign to Batch -> Auto-allocated to Students.

Homework & Tests Engine

Agenda Highlights
Upload Homework with PDF/Image attachments.
Digitally entry test scores and auto-update parent view.

Quick Setup Workflow

Follow these 5 steps to get your institute up and running in minutes.

1
Institute Profile

Configure your institute name, logo, domain, and contact details in the Admin Settings.

2
Recruit Staff

Add Teachers, Receptionists, and Principals. Assign them their roles and login credentials.

3
Define Academic Structure

Create your Batches and Subjects. This is the foundation of the student record system.

4
Enroll Students

Manually add students or import them into their respective batches.

5
Appoint Class Teachers

Assign teachers to their managed batches to unlock the Attendance and Analytics modules.
